Welcome to Tap & Timber

Pull up a stool, bring your friends and enjoy good times and eats at Tap & Timber Social. We're your neighborhood spot where the atmosphere is as inviting as the menu.

Located at 963 Sackville Drive, we pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming space where friends become family and every visit feels like coming home.

From our carefully crafted menu to our selection of cold drafts, everything we do is designed to bring people together. Come experience the perfect blend of comfort, quality, and community.

Meet Willie - Our Furry Ambassador

Willie is a spirited 7-year-old Morkie on a mission to help other furry friends find safe, happy forever homes. That's why he created the famous Willie Dog - a delicious treat inspired by his favorite flavors like cheese and bacon.

For every Willie Dog sold, $1 is donated to the SPCA, helping ensure that animals waiting for adoption have everything they need to feel safe and loved.

Willie's journey from Bathurst, New Brunswick wasn't easy, but he found his forever home with us. Now he's determined to help other animals find theirs.

And if you're lucky, you might spot Willie himself during your visit! He loves to drop by, say hello, and maybe even snap a photo with fans enjoying his signature treat.
